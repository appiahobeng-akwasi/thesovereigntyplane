§01 · The flagship

Where each country sits

Substantive on the x-axis, formal on the y-axis. The dashed line is the coherence diagonal. Distance above is sovereignty theatre. Distance below is ad-hoc capability. Reference anchors shown in white-fill circles to flag they are calibration points, not part of the African sample.

Sovereignty Theatre
Strategies and laws accumulate faster than the capacity to enforce, evaluate, or renegotiate.
Negotiated Interdependence
Coherent architecture with operational capacity. Two African cases: Kenya, South Africa.
Ad-Hoc Capability
Real delivery without scaffolding. No African case in the cohort sits cleanly here.
Dependency by Default
Honest about its starting point. Cameroon, Gabon, Sierra Leone, Ethiopia, Botswana, Malawi.
Cohort headline

Twelve of fifteen African states sit at gaps of nineteen percentage points or higher. Reference anchors cluster at the rubric ceiling: 95-100 formal, 80-88 substantive.
